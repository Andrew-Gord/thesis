Resources
Career Engagement
Opportunity CEO

The CEO is a group that helps every NCF student learn more about Career opportunities and how to succeed in the post-grad world. If you need help writing your resume or finding internships, they can help.

Student Success Center SSC

The SSC is a group that helps students with an assortment of resources, such as meetings with a personal coach or having group study sessions.

Writing Resource Center WRC

The WRC is where you should go if you’re facing any type of writing trouble, or if you just want someone to read through what you wrote and give feedback.

Counselling & Wellness
Center CWC

The CWC is for any health based crisis you may be facing.

Library Library

The library is a great place to go to relax or study. This link brings you to the library’s website which lets you look for online resources such as academic papers.

Academic Resource
Center ARC

The ARC is located on the first floor of the library and is where you can meet many resource groups such as the WRC.

Accessible Learning
Center ALC

ALC works collaboratively with faculty, staff and students to ensure that appropriate accommodations, services, resources and referrals are made that provide equity both inside and outside of the classroom.
